## $BTC and the market overall

The price action of **BTC** so far in June has been both very sad and essentially relying on Saylor. On Monday June 1st, **Strategy** announced that they had sold 32 BTC, which was the first BTC sale since late 2022. The price started dumping from there at $73k, and went down only for one week to $59k, which marked a local bottom again.

BTC then moved back up to $67k, but has now dumped to $62k again. The main focus of the market right now is on **STRC**, which has been having its largest drawdown ever in June. It dumped from $100 to $91 in early June when BTC had its dump to $60k, but surprisingly, while BTC hasn’t made new lows at all, STRC has been ‘collapsing’ over a week, and it even hit a low at $83 today during peak FUD. It think that this was likely to be the local bottom for STRC, unless BTC goes much further down. I tweet a lot about STRC, so if you want to read all my thoughts on the matter, you can find them [on Twitter](https://x.com/thedefivillain).

On the tradfi front, an **interim U.S.–Iran peace/ceasefire deal** was announced on June 14–15. ## Perp DEXes are doing well: $HYPE and $LIT

**$HYPE** has been extremely strong during the second half of May, and that move culminated with a new ATH at $76 in early June. Among the catalysts that helped it pump, there were the HYPE ETFs buying, the PURR DAT buying, and the incoming SPCX IPO, given the fact that a pre-IPO contract was trading on Hyperliquid. HYPE sold off to $52 along with BTC, but then bounced hard recently to sweep its highs and reach $77, which does now look like a double top.

## AI tokens: WLD mostly but also TAO VVV GRASS

AI tokens remain a narrative that is exciting for market participants. WLD has been the leading one in June.

- Surprisingly, **$WLD** has been among the strongest coins so far in June, and has pumped roughly 2x, to $7bn FDV at its top. The main catalyst for WLD seems to be the OpenAI IPO, but it’s probably not even happening in 2026, so I’m surprised it has such a strong impact on price. The second catalyst is that there’s going to be a tokenomics improvement in July with a decrease in the size of unlocks, so the selling pressure should get reduced. Overall, I tend to think that WLD is mostly a short around here.

- On June 13th, the US announced that the Mythos/Fable model from Anthropic was banned outside of the US. It immediately kickstarted a run on **$TAO**, as the main “decentralized AI play”. It pumped almost +40% but has been bleeding since this local top.
- **$VVV** finally seems to be stalling after its massive 20x pump from the bottom. For the first time in 2026, it’s been printing lower highs in June, after a new ATH at almost $22 in early June. VVV temporarily pumped on the Mythos ban too.
- **$GRASS** has been having higher lows since February. So far it’s been rejected at the $0.5 resistance again, but it still looks okay in the grand scheme of things. GRASS has its next token holder call on July 7.

## A potential ZEC exploit leads to a crash

On June 4, it got announced that there was potentially a vulnerability in **Zcash** that could have theoretically allowed someone to mint an infinite amount of **$ZEC** tokens. The news caused a massive crash in ZEC over one day, as it dumped -60% from $620 to a local bottom at $250. Fortunately for ZEC holder, that peak panic day marked a decisive bottom, and it bounced quite strongly from there, back to almost $500 in just a few days.

## Two main scam coins: $LAB and $BEAT

There have been two main scam coins in June so far: **$LAB** and **$BEAT**.

**$LAB** has been having a crazy run to $25bn FDV, making it one of the largest scale crime coins ever, on par with $RAVE, etc. Most of its final run has happened with very negative funding rate. It has had a correction down to $7, but it’s now already back to $15, meaning $15bn FDV… Until people will be able to unlock some of their tokens.

**$BEAT** had a massive rise to $5bn FDV after it launched in late 2025, but then sold off to $150M FDV in February 2026. From there, it slowly went up 7x until early June, and then skyrocketed from $1.2bn to $11bn FDV just a few days ago. It dumped from there and is currently trading at $1.8bn FDV.
